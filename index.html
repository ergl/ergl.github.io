Hi!

I’m Borja de Régil. I’m currently a software engineer at Ditto working on a distributed database powered by CRDTs. Before that, I was a distributed systems research engineer at the IMDEA Software Institute. Head over to the about section if you wish to know more, or want to check out things I’ve worked on.

You can find some of my thoughts on the rarely-updated blog. I might have changed my mind about some of the things written there, but I hope you can find something useful. I also keep (or try to) a personal wiki, where I keep looser notes on books and articles I read.

Feel free to reach out by email or Telegram, or check out my Github account. I’m always open to ideas, comments, or just general electronic banter.
