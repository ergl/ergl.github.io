Hi!

I’m Borja de Régil. I’m currently a research engineer at the IMDEA Software Institute working on distributed systems with Alexey Gotsman. Head over to the about section if you wish to know more, or want to check out things I’ve worked on.

You can find some of my opinions on the rarely-updated blog. Opinions are mostly wrong all the time, but I hope you can find some of them useful. I also keep (or try to) a personal wiki, where I keep looser notes on books and articles I read, and even more opinions I might or might not have anymore.

I don’t do much social media, but I do have a Github account. I tried to use Twitter two times in my life and both times I realized it was not for me. However, feel free to reach out by email or Telegram, I’m always open to ideas, comments, or just general electronic banter.
